Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and process

How long does it take to complete a project?
Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex applications can take 2-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
What is your payment structure?
I typically work with a 50% upfront payment to begin the project, with the remaining 50% due upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. I accept bank transfers, PayPal, and other payment methods.
Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?
Yes, I provide 30 days of free support after project launch to handle any bugs or minor adjustments. After that, I offer monthly maintenance packages or hourly support as needed.
Can you work with my existing website?
Absolutely! I can work with existing websites to improve performance, add new features, or completely redesign them. I'll assess your current setup and recommend the best approach for your needs.
What technologies do you specialize in?
I specialize in modern web technologies including React, Next.js, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and PostgreSQL. For mobile development, I use React Native. I'm also proficient in UI/UX design tools like Figma and Adobe XD.
