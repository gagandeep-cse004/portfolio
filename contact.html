Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

What is your typical project timeline?
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex applications can take 2-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
What is your payment structure?
I typically work with a 50% upfront payment to begin, with the remaining 50% due upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. I accept bank transfers, PayPal, and major credit cards.
Do you offer ongoing support?
Yes! I provide 30 days of free support after project launch for any bugs or minor adjustments. After that, I offer monthly maintenance packages starting at $299/month or hourly support as needed.
Do you work with clients remotely?
Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide. Most of my projects are handled remotely using tools like Zoom, Slack, and project management software. I'm flexible with time zones and can accommodate different schedules.
What technologies do you specialize in?
I specialize in React, Next.js, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, and React Native for mobile development. For design, I use Figma and Adobe XD. I'm always learning new technologies to stay current.
Can you work with my existing team?
Yes, I frequently collaborate with in-house teams, designers, and other developers. I'm comfortable using Git, Jira, Asana, and other collaboration tools. I can adapt to your team's workflow and processes.
